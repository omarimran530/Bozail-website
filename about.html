About Bozail

Experience meets efficiency in global trade and logistics.

Our Story

Bozail International was founded to redefine import/export through speed, quality, and unmatched supplier networks.

Our team has decades of experience sourcing agriculture and mineral products, aligning traditional trade methods with modern logistics and digital communication.

Our Mission

To serve as a trusted intermediary in global commodity trade, helping connect suppliers and buyers with clarity and assurance.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Transparent and ethical at every step.
  • Agility: Fast responses to market shifts and client needs.
  • Precision: Timely delivery with accurate documentation and logistics.

What We Offer

Global Sourcing

We maintain strong supplier networks in Asia, Africa, and South America to ensure timely procurement of commodities like petcoke, copper ore, soybeans, and pulses.

Custom Logistics

Door-to-port and port-to-door service with verified freight providers, customs handlers, and tracking.

Bulk Trade Consulting

Whether you're a startup or an enterprise, we help structure and execute your first trade transaction from inquiry to execution.
