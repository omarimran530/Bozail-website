About Bozail

Bozail is an import-export enterprise connecting agricultural and industrial markets worldwide. With deep ties to producers in South Asia, Africa, and China, our mission is to ensure high-quality trade with transparency and efficiency.

Founded by a family with over 20 years of international trade experience, Bozail handles everything from grains and minerals to electronics and chipsets.
